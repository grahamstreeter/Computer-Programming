  • The International Day of Peace is Thursday, 9/21. The Peace Day Challenge is a global effort to turn the International Day of Peace into a day of global action that affirms peace as a real alternative to the increasing violence we see daily. We invite you to commit to the Peace Day Challenge by taking an action on or around 9/21 and sharing it on your social media accounts, using the tag #PeaceDayChallenge. At CVU, stop by our table in the Cafe on MONDAY, 9/18 and WEDNESDAY, 9/20 to fill out a Shout-Out For Peace note in recognition of a Peacebuilder, conflict resolver, or source of peace inspiration in your life or in the world. We will share these Shout-Outs throughout the school on Thursday!
